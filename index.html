Yo-Yo Lin - Object & Method Lecture

Kenetic Poster Design for Yoyo Lin's lecture on zoom, April 2022.

Loop GIFs, 00:07.

This poster is designed for the Object and Method lecture of Yo-Yo Lin 林友友, a Taiwanese-American, interdisciplinary media artist, at VCUarts Graphic Design program.
This poster was mainly inspired by Yo-Yo's work Re: collections / 再次·回顧. The film is based on her return to her motherland in Taiwan during the pandemic and talks about the moment when the artist's memories and experiences across the two lands intersect.

The elements of this poster include video clips of ants working, and a kinetic type design of Yo-Yo Lin's name in both English and traditional Chinese(林友友) in a motion that combines and then separates.

Poster, July 2022. 29.7 x 42 cm

Peace is the soft clouds, the quietness of blue, the moisture of the rain, and the comfort and usual days. Greedy and war covertly hide behind the pacific days that we take for granted. The clouds are the word “peace” spelled in the Morse code, which is widely used during the war. All the dots and dashes are burnt holes of paper. The blue covers the burnt color of the edges. The raindrop graphics cross out the dates of the Ukraine war Russo-Ukrainian War. The war is still occurring after we sign numerous declarations of peace.

Voice

Poster, 72.8 x 103 cm.

Not until I left my home country that I realize how voice can bring a sense of belonging. When I felt homesick in the United States, I would listen to the recording of the daily conversation between myself and people in my home country, and I could imagine I was at home. Voice is about our experience, culture, thoughts, and language. This poster of the graphical notation is created to record the voices around me that I grew up with and to make these voices tangible to every viewer.The main materials of the poster are four recordings of the daily conversation between myself and the people in my neighborhood. It is designed to be B1 size with an ivory background like the Suzuki sheet music and green notations that stands for the Taiwan image in my mind.
